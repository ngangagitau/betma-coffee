From Farm to Cup

Founded in 2015, BETMA COFFEE began as a simple idea: to share exceptional coffee while building meaningful relationships with farmers and communities at origin.

Our journey started when founders Maria and Ben Thompson traveled through Central America, discovering not just incredible coffee, but the passionate people who cultivate it. They witnessed firsthand the challenges farmers face and the difference direct trade could make.

Today, we work directly with over 20 smallholder farms across 8 countries, ensuring fair prices, sustainable practices, and exceptional quality in every bag we roast.

Our Sourcing Philosophy

Building a better coffee future, one relationship at a time

Direct & Transparent

We believe in knowing exactly where our coffee comes from and who grows it. That's why we've built direct relationships with farmers, visiting origins multiple times each year.

Our transparent pricing model means farmers receive premium payments that reflect quality and support community development projects like schools, healthcare, and environmental conservation.

Through our Direct Impact Program, we've helped fund the planting of over 50,000 shade trees and supported education for 200+ children in coffee-growing communities.
